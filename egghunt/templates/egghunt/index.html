The concept

How the game works

The eEaster Egg Hunt is a treasure hunt. Picture clues point to a location somewhere on Earth. You can investigate a location by placing a pin on a map. If you were right, you'll retrieve the next clue.

The details

To search for an egg, place a pin on the map. If you are within the accepted radius of the true location, you will move on to the next clue. Otherwise, you may need to drop your pin closer to the egg's location, or think about the clue agian. There's no need to save your progress because all clues are live at the same time, so you just need to remember the last egg location that you found.

The clues are separated into two levels. Level 1 should be accessible to people with a good knowledge of the world. Level 2 is more difficult and use of Google is probably necessary. Using a similar-image search is cheating. Anything which you feel is probably cheating, is cheating.
