eEaster Egg Hunt

Follow a trail of clues across a wild continent

Welcome

The eEaster Egg Hunt returns for 2020! This year's hunt takes you to the mythical continent of Pazira where you embark on a quest to find the cure to a life-threatening disease by solving riddles, translating hieroglyphics and using your wits. Happy Easter!

How to play

The eEasterEgg Hunt works like any other. You start with a clue. By solving the clue, you can continue the story, until you reach the next clue.

Solving the clues

The clues can be difficult and you're not expected to "just know it". A little persistence will be needed! Unlike previous versions of the eEaster Egg hunt, the answers to this year's clues are in the form of text. For every clue, your answer needs to exactly match one of the accepted answers. If you think you have the right answer but it is not accepted, try rephrasing or respelling your answer.

Resuming

You can pause and resume the hunt at any time. Just remember the solution to the last puzzle you solved. When you want to resume, enter that answer on the resume page .
